Drinks I Have Known & Loved

There are so many beverages on this earthly plane which we guzzle with little to no thought whatsoever. This website is an attempt to make sense of the maddening infinity of potable matter in the world.
Jesus turned water into wine, but I wish that he would've turned it back.

Water is one of my fave beverages. It's where all drinks start. I put the HO in H2O. Hydrogen and oxygen collide in a dazzling dance to create the wellspring of life. How crazy is that?

Coffee is my second love. Literally why I get up in the morning. I thank the powers that be for the crazy goats who first ate coffee cherries, and their sweet observant genius of a herder.

Tea is there to usher me into bedtime. Plus a seemingly limitless amount of kinds!! You can't beat hot leaf water at any time of day or any place in the world, am I right?
